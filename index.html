Intro

Xu Jin, Male, Born in Qian County, Shannxi Province, now is the Professor of Peking University, Supervisor of PhD Candidates.

Obtained his Doctor's Degree of Engineering Administration of Xi'an JiaoTong University under the supervisition of Chinese Academician Yingluo Wang in 1993.

Obtained the Doctor's Degree of Science in department of mathematics of Beijing Institute of Technology in 1995.

His postdoctoral research in circuit and signal processing was directed by Chinese Academician Bao Zheng in Xidian University.

He is the organizing committee member of International Biological Computer conference, the committee of Chinese Institute of Electronics-Circuits and Systems and the director of Chinese Institute of Electronics-Graph theory and System optimization.

In 1994, he was invited to the Department of mathematics of National University of Singapore for three academic reports.

in 1996, he visited the Chinese University of Hong Kong for half a year

in 1997 and 1998, he was invited to the Department of computer science of National University of Singapore as a researcher. In 2006 and 2010 he was chairman of international conference on biological computer twice in succession.
